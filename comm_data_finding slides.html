Relationship between BorrowerAPR and Employment status¶

Notes¶

The BorrowerAPR for unemployed indivituals is slightly higher than the rest since they have a higher risk of default.

Relation between BorrowerRate and Employment status¶

Notes¶

The highest BorrowerRate are paid by unemployed individuals followe closely by the retired and those doing part time jobs. This is because they tend not to have a regular stream of income thus are more exposed to defaulting the loans. This informs the reason why their rates are higher.

Relationship between BorrowerAPR and ProsperRating¶

Notes¶

Borrowers with AA Prosper rating tend to be charged a lower rate on their loans while those with HR Prosper rating are charged the highest rates on their loans due to their high risk of default.

Relationship between a Loan Amount and EmploymentStatus¶

Notes¶

As expected, individuals who are employed are awarded a large loan amount. This is simply because they have a regular sours of income and are therefore able to service their loans with ease as signed in the loan contract.

Establishing the relationship between Loan Amount and Borrower Rate.¶

We can clearly observe that the borrower rate charged on loans offered by Prosper is dependent on the loan amount.A larger loan amount attracts a smaller rate and there is an inverse relationship.
